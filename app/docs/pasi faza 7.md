Building Calculator - Walkthrough & Progress
Phase 7: Personal Library & Project Templates (NEW)
We have transformed the application from a project tracker into a Knowledge Powerhouse for builders. Developers can now capture their expertise and reuse it across multiple projects.

1. Personal Library Management
Save to Library: In the Estimate Editor, you can now clone any customized recipe (including special materials, labor rates, and waste percentages) into your personal Master Catalog.
My Library: The Catalog now includes a "Biblioteca Mea" filter, allowing you to instantly find your custom items versus standard system norms.
Master Item Creation: You can create new master items directly from the catalog for future use.
2. Project Templates
Save as Template: Capture a project's entire structure—including all stages and every detailed estimate line—as a reusable blueprint.
Zero-Friction Onboarding: When creating a new project, you can now select a template. The app will automatically pre-populate the dashboard with your best practices, recipes, and budget structures.
Phase 6: Cost & Cashflow Control Panel (MVP)
The application is now a professional decision support tool for small developers.

1. Financial Dashboard
Real-Time Visibility: Instant view of Spent vs. Remaining vs. Forecast.
Profit Tracking: Live recalculation of projected profit based on actual purchases and remaining budget.
Intelligent Alerts: Automatic warnings for stage budget overruns and margin risks.
2. Purchase Tracking
Streamlined Logging: Fast input for materials, labor, and equipment with auto-allocation to project stages.
Dynamic Recalculation: Every purchase entry instantly updates the entire financial engine of the project.
3. Smart Forecasting
30-Day Outlook: Automatically projects upcoming costs based on the project plan and current progress.
Proactive Planning: Helps developers avoid cashflow gaps before they happen.
Phase 5: Professional Recipes
Granular Control: Detailed breakdown of Labor, Materials, Equipment, and Transport for every item.
Live Overrides: Ability to customize recipes for specific project conditions without affecting the master catalog.
